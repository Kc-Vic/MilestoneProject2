Says Who

Guidelines

The game is simple. Guess the author of the quote.

You have 4 options to choose from. Click on the answer you think is correct.

You will get 1 point for each correct answer.

You will see your score tally after every question.

The game ends when you have answered every question.

You can play as many times as you want

Click on the start button to begin the game.
