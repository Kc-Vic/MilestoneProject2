Says Who

Guidelines

  • Each round presents you with a famous quote and four possible authors. Your mission? Identify who actually said it!.
  • Simply click on the name you believe is correct. Choose wisely!
  • A blurred image of the author appears with each quote. After you answer, the image becomes clear - giving you a face to match with the words.
  • Earn 1 point for each correct attribution. Watch your score grow with each right answer!
  • After answering, you'll see immediately if you were right or wrong.
  • The challenge consists of 15 fascinating quotes from historical figures, celebrities, and fictional characters.
  • Finished all quotes? Test your knowledge again with another round by clicking "Start Game"!
